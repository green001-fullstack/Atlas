# Foundation

## Product Name

ATLAS

## Elevator Pitch

Atlas is an explainable financial trust intelligence platform that helps lenders assess applicants with little or no traditional credit history using alternative financial behavior

## Mission

Atlas helps financial institutions make fair, transparent, and evidence-based lending decisions for people with little or no traditional credit history.

## Vision

A future where access to financial opportunity is determined by trustworthy financial behavior rather than the presence of traditional credit history.

## Product Values

### Transparency

Every recommendation should be explainable.

### Fairness

People should be evaluated based on evidence, not assumptions.

### Privacy

Financial data belongs to the user.

### Human Oversight

Humans always make the final lending decision.

### Inclusion

People without traditional credit histories deserve a fair opportunity to demonstrate financial trustworthiness.

### Continuous Improvement

Financial behavior changes. People should be able to improve their financial profile over time. 

## Product Principles

### Principle 1

Atlas assists decision-making.

It never replaces human judgment.

### Principle 2

Every recommendation must include an explanation.

### Principle 3

Predictions should communicate confidence, not certainty.

### Principle 4

No financial data is collected or processed without explicit user consent.

### Principle 5

The system should reduce financial exclusion while maintaining responsible risk assessment.

### Principle 6

Simple is better than clever.

If a feature makes Amina's(Loan officer) job harder, it doesn't belong.
